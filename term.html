Regulations

To ensure service quality, JobHive does not allow one user to create multiple different accounts.

If a violation is detected, JobHive will stop providing services to all duplicate accounts or block all access to the JobHive website system.

After registering a recruiter account and providing the necessary information, recruiters may be supported to display basic (standard) job postings, excluding certain positions. The number of job postings and how they are displayed depends on JobHive's regulations at each time.
